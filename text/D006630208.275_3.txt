CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

Based on the foregoing findings of fact, Disciplinary Panel A concludes as a matter _
of law that the Respondent is guilty of unprofessional conduct in the practice of medicine,
in violation of Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(3)(ii); is professionally, physically, or mentally
incompetent, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(4); and provides professional services
while under the influence of alcohol, in violation of Health Occ. § 14-404(a)(9)(i).
