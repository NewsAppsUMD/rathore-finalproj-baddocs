CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

Panel A finds Dr. Lizardi in default based upon his failure to appear or participate
at the Prehearing Conference on January 30, 2024, at OAH. See State Gov’t § 10-210(4).
Panel A finds, under Health Occ. § 14-205(b)(3)(i), reasons that are grounds for the denial
of Dr. Lizardi’s application for medical licensure in Maryland under Health Occ. § 14-404.

country for an act that would be grounds for disciplinary action, in violation of Health Occ.
