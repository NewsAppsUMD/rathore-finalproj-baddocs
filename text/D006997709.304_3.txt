11. On or about August 20, 2024, the Respondent was referred to another
specialist for an evaluation. Based on the evaluation, the specialist concluded the
Respondent “is not found fit to continue in his position as psychiatrist [at the Hospital].”°

12. On September 6, 2024, a specialist retained by the Program conducted a
comprehensive evaluation of the Respondent to determine his present fitness to practice
medicine safely. The specialist issued a report that concluded the Respondent “is not
currently competent to practice psychiatric medicine.”

CONCLUSION OF LAW

Based upon the foregoing Investigative Findings, Panel A of the Board concludes
as a matter of law that the public health, safety, or welfare imperatively requires
emergency action, and that pursuant to State Gov’t § 10-226(c)(2) and COMAR
10.32.02.08B(7), the Respondent’s license is summarily suspended.
